# Find Pitch Opportunities

Identify opportunities for media pitching, podcast appearances, speaking engagements, and reactive commentary.

## Prerequisites

Read:
- `context/brand/brand-overview.md`
- `context/brand/messaging-framework.md`
- `context/audiences/primary-audiences.md`
- `context/strategy/key-messages.md`

## Opportunity Types

### 1. Podcast Opportunities
Find podcasts where spokesperson could appear:
- Industry-relevant podcasts
- Business/leadership podcasts
- Audience-aligned podcasts

### 2. Speaking Opportunities
Identify conferences, events, webinars:
- Industry conferences
- Business events
- Webinar series
- Panels and roundtables

### 3. Reactive Pitching
Identify newsjacking opportunities:
- Breaking industry news
- Trending topics
- Policy/regulatory changes
- Competitor news
- Seasonal moments

## Research Process

Use the `media-list-builder` agent to:

1. **Podcast Research**
   - Search for relevant podcasts by topic
   - Evaluate audience fit
   - Check guest booking process
   - Prioritize by reach and relevance

2. **Event Research**
   - Search for upcoming conferences
   - Identify CFP deadlines
   - Evaluate audience alignment
   - Check speaker requirements

3. **Reactive Monitoring**
   - Scan current news
   - Check trending topics
   - Identify commentary angles
   - Assess timing urgency
