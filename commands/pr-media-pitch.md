# Create Media Pitch

Develop a compelling media pitch for a story angle.

## Prerequisites

Read:
- `context/brand/brand-overview.md`
- `context/brand/messaging-framework.md`
- `context/historical/media-coverage.md`
- Any relevant materials in `context/` or `inputs/`

## Pitch Brief

Gather from user:
1. **Story angle**: What's the news or story?
2. **News hook**: Why now? What makes it timely?
3. **Target outlets/reporters**: Who to pitch?
4. **Spokesperson**: Who can speak to this?
5. **Exclusive or broad**: Single outlet or wide distribution?
6. **Supporting materials**: Press release, data, visuals available?

## Pitch Development

Use the `media-relations-advisor` agent to develop the pitch.

### Pitch Components:
1. **Subject line**: Compelling, specific, not clickbait
2. **Personalized opening**: Why this reporter/outlet
3. **The hook**: Story in 1-2 sentences
4. **Relevance**: Why their audience cares
5. **Key details**: What, who, when, where
6. **The ask**: Interview, exclusive, comment
7. **Credentials**: Why source is credible
8. **Contact info**: How to respond

### Quality Criteria:
- Newsworthy and timely
- Clear value for outlet's audience
- Appropriate tone for journalist
- Concise (under 200 words)
- Single clear ask

## Output

Provide:
1. Ready-to-send pitch email
2. Suggested follow-up approach
3. Talking points for spokesperson (if interview likely)

Save to: `outputs/pitches/[date]-pitch-[topic].md`

## Completion

1. Review pitch with user
2. Suggest any supporting materials needed
3. Offer interview prep if relevant
4. Commit pitch file
