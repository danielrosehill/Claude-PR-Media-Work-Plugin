# Competitive Scan

Analyze competitors' communications strategies and positioning.

## Prerequisites

Read:
- `context/competitive/competitor-profiles.md`
- `context/competitive/market-position.md`
- `context/brand/messaging-framework.md`

## Scan Parameters

Gather from user:
1. **Competitors**: Which competitors to analyze
2. **Scope**: Broad scan or deep dive
3. **Channels**: Which channels to examine
4. **Focus areas**: Messaging, content, media, social, all

## Analysis Framework

### Communications Audit by Competitor

**Positioning & Messaging**
- How do they position themselves?
- What are their key messages?
- What proof points do they use?
- How do they differentiate?

**Content Strategy**
- What content types do they produce?
- What themes/topics do they cover?
- How frequently do they publish?
- What quality level?

**Social Presence**
- Which platforms are they active on?
- What's their voice/tone?
- How much engagement do they get?
- What content performs well?

**Media Relations**
- What coverage do they receive?
- What publications cover them?
- What stories get picked up?
- Who are their spokespeople?

**Share of Voice**
- How do mention volumes compare?
- What sentiment do they receive?
- Where are they strongest?

### Comparative Analysis

**Strengths & Weaknesses**
- Where is each competitor strong?
- Where are they weak?
- What are they doing well that we could learn from?
- What are they doing poorly that we can exploit?

**Whitespace Opportunities**
- What messages aren't being claimed?
- What audiences aren't being served?
- What channels aren't being utilized?
- What content isn't being created?
