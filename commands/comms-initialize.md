# Initialize Communications Workspace

You are initializing this workspace for a new communications engagement. Conduct an interactive interview to gather essential information and populate the context files.

## Interview Process

Conduct this interview conversationally, gathering information in logical phases. Be thorough but efficient.

### Phase 1: Client/Brand Overview

Ask about:
1. **Organization basics**
   - Company/individual name
   - Industry/sector
   - Size and stage (startup, growth, enterprise, individual)
   - Geographic presence

2. **Mission and positioning**
   - What does the organization do?
   - What problem do they solve?
   - What makes them different from competitors?
   - Mission/vision/values (if established)

3. **Current situation**
   - Why are they investing in communications now?
   - What's working in their current communications?
   - What's not working?
   - Any recent events or changes driving this?

### Phase 2: Communications Goals

Ask about:
1. **Business objectives**
   - What business outcomes should communications support?
   - What does success look like in 6-12 months?

2. **Communications objectives**
   - Awareness, perception, lead generation, thought leadership?
   - What do they want people to know, think, feel, or do?

3. **Constraints**
   - Budget considerations
   - Resource constraints (team size, capabilities)
   - Timeline pressures
   - Any sensitivities or restrictions

### Phase 3: Audiences

Ask about:
1. **Primary audiences**
   - Who are the most important people to reach?
   - What do they need from these audiences?
   - What do these audiences currently think/believe?

2. **Secondary audiences**
   - Other important stakeholder groups
   - Influencers on primary audiences

3. **Media landscape**
   - Relevant publications and journalists
   - Industry analysts or influencers
   - Any existing media relationships

### Phase 4: Brand Voice & Messaging

Ask about:
1. **Brand personality**
   - How should the brand sound? (formal/casual, serious/playful, etc.)
   - What adjectives describe the ideal brand voice?
   - Any brands they admire for communications style?

2. **Existing messaging**
   - Current tagline or positioning statement
   - Key messages they've been using
   - Proof points and evidence they have

3. **Competitive differentiation**
   - Main competitors
   - How they differentiate from competitors
   - What they don't want to be associated with

### Phase 5: Historical Context

Ask about:
1. **Past communications activities**
   - What communications have they done before?
   - What worked well?
   - What didn't work?

2. **Existing materials**
   - Website, blog, social presence
   - Press coverage history
   - Marketing collateral

3. **Performance data**
   - Any metrics they currently track
   - Baselines for key metrics
   - Historical performance

## Documentation

After completing the interview, create/update the following files:

### Core Profile
**File**: `context/client-profile.md`
- Organization name and overview
- Industry and market position
- Key contacts and stakeholders
- Engagement scope and constraints

### Brand Context
**Files in**: `context/brand/`
- `brand-overview.md`: Mission, vision, values, positioning
- `messaging-framework.md`: Key messages, proof points, differentiators
- `voice-and-tone.md`: Voice attributes, tone guidelines

### Audience Context
**Files in**: `context/audiences/`
- `primary-audiences.md`: Detailed primary audience profiles
- `secondary-audiences.md`: Secondary audiences
- `stakeholder-map.md`: Key stakeholders

### Strategic Context
**Files in**: `context/strategy/`
- `goals-and-objectives.md`: Business and communications objectives
- `communications-strategy.md`: Initial strategic framework
- `key-messages.md`: Approved key messages

### Competitive Context
**Files in**: `context/competitive/`
- `competitor-profiles.md`: Key competitors
- `market-position.md`: Current positioning

### Historical Context
**Files in**: `context/historical/`
- `campaign-history.md`: Past activities
- `performance-benchmarks.md`: Baseline metrics

## Completion

After populating context files:
1. Summarize the engagement overview
2. Highlight any gaps or areas needing further research
3. Suggest immediate next steps
4. Recommend which workflows to start with
