# Create Campaign

Develop a comprehensive communications campaign framework.

## Prerequisites

Read context files:
- `context/strategy/goals-and-objectives.md`
- `context/strategy/communications-strategy.md`
- `context/audiences/primary-audiences.md`
- `context/brand/messaging-framework.md`
- `context/brand/voice-and-tone.md`
- `context/historical/campaign-history.md`

Check for campaign brief in `inputs/briefs/`

## Campaign Development Process

### 1. Campaign Brief

Gather or confirm:
- Campaign name/working title
- Business objective driving this campaign
- Specific campaign objective (measurable)
- Target audiences
- Key messages to convey
- Available budget and resources
- Timeline and key dates
- Success metrics

### 2. Strategic Foundation

Develop:
- Campaign positioning
- Creative concept/big idea
- Messaging hierarchy
- Audience prioritization

### 3. Channel Strategy

Plan:
- Channel mix (PESO model)
- Channel-specific objectives
- Content types by channel
- Sequencing and timing

### 4. Content Plan

Define:
- Content pillars
- Hero, hub, hygiene content
- Content calendar framework
- Asset requirements

### 5. Tactical Plan

Detail:
- Specific activities by channel
- Timeline with milestones
- Responsibilities (if applicable)
- Dependencies

### 6. Measurement Framework

Establish:
- Primary KPIs
- Secondary metrics
- Measurement methodology
- Reporting cadence
- Optimization triggers

## Output

Use the `campaign-strategist` agent to develop the full strategy.

Create campaign folder:
```
outputs/campaigns/[campaign-name]/
├── strategy/
│   └── campaign-strategy.md
├── content/
├── social/
├── press/
└── performance/
```

Save campaign strategy to `outputs/campaigns/[campaign-name]/strategy/campaign-strategy.md`

## Completion

1. Review complete campaign strategy
2. Identify any gaps or decisions needed
3. Outline immediate next steps
4. Commit campaign framework
