# Generate Social Media Ideas

Generate social media content ideas aligned with strategy and brand voice.

## Prerequisites

Read:
- `context/brand/voice-and-tone.md`
- `context/brand/messaging-framework.md`
- `context/strategy/key-messages.md`
- `context/audiences/primary-audiences.md`

## Ideation Parameters

Gather from user:
1. **Platforms**: Which social platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
2. **Timeframe**: Content for what period (week, month, quarter)
3. **Themes/Topics**: Any specific themes or topics to focus on
4. **Content Mix**: Balance of promotional, educational, engaging content
5. **Key Dates**: Relevant events, holidays, launches to incorporate
6. **Campaigns**: Any active campaigns to support

## Ideation Framework

### Content Pillars
Map ideas to content pillars:
- Thought leadership
- Product/service highlights
- Behind-the-scenes/culture
- Customer stories
- Industry trends
- Educational content
- Community engagement

### Content Formats
Consider variety:
- Text posts
- Images/graphics
- Videos
- Polls/questions
- Carousels
- Stories/ephemeral content
- Live content

### Engagement Hooks
Ideas that drive engagement:
- Questions and polls
- Controversial/debate starters
- Trending topics
- Interactive content
- User-generated content prompts

## Output Format

Generate ideas in this format:

```markdown
# Social Media Content Ideas

## Overview
- **Period**: [Timeframe]
- **Platforms**: [Platforms]
- **Theme Focus**: [Themes]

## Content Ideas

### [Theme/Pillar]

#### Idea 1: [Concept Title]
- **Platform(s)**: [Where to post]
- **Format**: [Text/image/video/etc.]
- **Hook**: [Opening line or visual concept]
- **Key Message**: [Message to convey]
- **CTA**: [Desired action]
- **Notes**: [Additional context]

[Repeat for each idea]
```
