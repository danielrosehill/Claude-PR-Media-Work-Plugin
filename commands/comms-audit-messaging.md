# Audit Messaging

Review communications materials for messaging consistency and brand alignment.

## Prerequisites

Read:
- `context/brand/messaging-framework.md`
- `context/brand/voice-and-tone.md`
- `context/strategy/key-messages.md`

## Audit Scope

Gather from user:
1. **Materials to audit**: What content to review
2. **Focus areas**: Voice, messaging, both
3. **Depth**: Quick scan or detailed review

## Audit Process

Use the `brand-voice-guardian` agent for detailed review.

### Review Dimensions:

**Message Alignment**
- Are key messages present?
- Are messages accurate and current?
- Is competitive differentiation clear?
- Are proof points used correctly?

**Voice Consistency**
- Does language match brand personality?
- Is vocabulary appropriate?
- Is tone right for audience/channel?

**Quality Issues**
- Any factual inaccuracies?
- Outdated information?
- Inconsistencies across materials?
- Grammar/style issues?
